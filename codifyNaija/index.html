Our Patnerships

In 2015, we have patnered with three non-governmental organizations namely: Paradigm Nigeria Initiative, France-based Digital Aid, and Rochas Foundation College Kano, to organize a programming camp for students. This project entailed teaching software design and implementation to secondary school students. Please visit code camp for more details

Digital Aid

Digital Aid is a France based NGO and recycling agency. They donated IT equipment in support of our first project.

Paradigm Initiative Nigeria (PIN)

PIN is a social entreprise that connects Nigerian youths with ICT opportunities in Nigeria. PIN co-organized our first program for teaching software engineering to secondary school students in Kano.

Rochas Foundation College Kano

A subsidiary of the Rochas Foundation group of schools. This college hosted our first code camp at no cost.

Our programmes

We offer courses on software engineering and design for students and professionals. We also organize code camps at different times of the year. Our areas of expertise include software design and implementation in Java, C++, Python, Javascript, and web design in Html and Css.
